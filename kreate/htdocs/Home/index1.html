WELCOME TO KREATE TECHNOLOGY

Summery




Kreate technology were we stive to be the best in the the technology industry Like all great innovations RealTIME was built out of necessity. Specifically, it was designed to help a creative team cope with positive business growth, differentiate from competitors and become more efficient and profitable.

After investigating the many project management packages available in the market and realizing none of them were designed with creatives in mind, OneKreate, a thriving creative agency, realized that there was a gap in the marketplace. All of the existing products were complex, had legacy interfaces that creatives would not embrace, and relied on old technologies that were increasingly incompatible with the modern mobile-centric world. Simply put, they did not serve the needs of the creative community and required too much behavior modification for successful adoption.

Products We Provide

Manage

MANAGE is the project management hub of the RealTime Suite, enabling creation and management of all the Marketing initiatives in your organization. In MANAGE, each member of your creative team has a personalized dashboard displaying their prioritized deliverables.What makes RealTime MANAGE different from other project management tools is the integration of other RealTime modules directly into the project work flow. So your team members can not only see their tasks, they can complete those tasks within the RealTime Suite itself. Because RealTime is built on the latest web technologies, it can be accessed for any device with a standard browser, without downloading plug-ins or client applications. So your team can work from anywhere and never miss a beat.

Approve

With RealTime APPROVE, your team can:

Make selections, approvals and rejections for any asset Mark-up assets with detailed notes visible by other reviewers View multiple versions of the same image to compare changes Work on single or grouped assets to speed review APPROVE provides a structured workflow for moving creative content through a collaborative review and/or selection process. Built-in mark-up and comment tools enable reviewers to indicate their specific changes both visually and in associated comments. Once the collaboration process is complete, APPROVE passes the content back to MANAGE for additional workflow steps such as a project manager review or hand in to the design team, depending on your organizations specific process.

Share

With RealTime SHARE, your team can:

  • Manage an unlimited number of assets via a simple web-based interface
  • Add metadata simply and easily to enable more accurate asset search
  • Navigate to assets via a virtual folder structure supporting taxonomy by business unit/team
  • Provide links to specific asset/s for external partners that do not have direct system access

Transform assets into various forms on the fly for direct download Push content directly to web, social or other channels via custom automation SHARE is a simple and powerful Digital Asset Management System enabling access to all creative imagery, graphic design elements, video clips and other content once they have been created and approved. The intuitive web based interface and powerful search function lets marketing teams easily and and download any asset in any size, resolution or file format from their desktop.This permission-based system can be shared with entire organizations, agency partners or with select teams as desired. The integrated reporting tool gives managers visibility to who has downloaded which assets, at what time and in what format.

Track

With RealTime TRACK, your team can:

  • Manage an unlimited number of products in multiple locations via a simple web based interface
  • create shot lists and groupings of products to be shot
  • Update and review product status (damage, returns, in-transit, etc.)
  • Generate reports to analyze efficiency
  • Scan and Generate tracking labels to monitor products in the production pipeline

    • TRACK was developed to better manage vendor product samples and prototypes. With RealTime TRACK, from the moment a product sample or prototype arrives on the dock you can manage its lifecycle and ensure it moves through the production pipeline efficiently. TRACK also supports creation of shot lists and groupings to quickly plan photo or video shoots, and provides an array of reporting options for management review.
